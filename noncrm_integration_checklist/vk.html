WHAT DOES THE B BADGE MEAN?
B = Bridge Node — a term from the RLM (Recursive Latent Mapping) algorithm. A bridge node is a blocker whose resolution unblocks the greatest number of downstream items simultaneously. Think of it as a keystone: remove it and many other blockers automatically clear. Bridge nodes are identified by computing the fan-out coefficient — the count of nodes that become resolvable once this one is resolved.
